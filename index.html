About

At the age of 16, I heard, for the first time, Chopin's Nocturne Op9-N01, and fell in love with the peice. At the time, I had no idea what music was or how to play an instrument.

Then, by chance, I met with my first piano teacher Riad Elias, and I started practicing how to play the piano with him. Within a short period of time, I finished four levels. Then I went on to study under Iraqi-Czech concert pianist, Natasha Al Radi, where I spent three years learning and mastering techniques, elevating how to present music to audiences.

After I continued my music studies at École Antoinine in Lebanon where I earned my diploma. I also studied piano with Proffessor Armen Kachik, head of the piano department, and harmony and music composition with the director of the school, Dr. Fadi Tawk. Along with the previously mentioned, I took private classes in conducting and Orchestration with maestro Garo Avessian.
